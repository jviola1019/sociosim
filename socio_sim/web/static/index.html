    Power an incremental-lift RCT before you run it. Sizing at 80% power, α=0.05 (Cohen 1988); incrementality needs an RCT holdout, not last-touch (Gordon et al. 2019).

    Holdout 10% default, 10–20% preferred (derive from this power calc). Reality: ad RCTs are hard to power — sales variance is high (Lewis & Rao 2015).

    ROAS, CAC and LTV:CAC with cited guidance. These are business inputs, not measured returns — pair with an incrementality test.
