# Society & Policy Lab

The Society & Policy Lab compares synthetic platform-rule, moderation, feed,
advertising, and governance assumptions. Results describe modeled behavior
inside SocioSim only.

Current executable controls include jurisdictions, policy packs, classifier
operating points, feed strategy, harmful-content base rates, EU opt-out proxy,
ad restrictions, red-team archetypes, human-review delay, appeal assumptions,
and graph dynamics.

Required wording for results:

- "Within this configured synthetic model..."
- "The modeled outcome changed by..."
- "This is not a real-world forecast."

Do not write that a policy will reduce harm, comply with law, or improve a real
platform. Policy packs are research approximations and not legal advice.
