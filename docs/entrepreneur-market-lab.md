# Entrepreneur & Market Lab

The Entrepreneur & Market Lab is a fictional market scenario workflow. It
creates synthetic campaign configurations and holdout comparisons; it is not a
real advertising optimizer.

Supported executable controls include ad enablement, FTC disclosure behavior,
budget and bid through campaign specs, frequency cap, holdout fraction,
synthetic conversion parameters, jurisdictional ad restrictions, and feed
strategy.

Every monetary result must display:

`Synthetic scenario input/output. Not a forecast of real financial performance.`

Use holdout lift and BH-corrected q-values as within-simulation diagnostics.
Never present synthetic ROAS, CAC, LTV:CAC, or lift as actual ROI.
